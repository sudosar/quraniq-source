# 🕌 QuranIQ

**A Daily Puzzle Challenge to Deepen Your Connection with the Quran.**

Welcome to QuranIQ! This is an interactive daily puzzle game designed to help you engage with the Holy Quran in a fun, meaningful, and consistent way. Test your knowledge, learn new verses, and build a daily habit through our unique game modes.

[**👉 Play QuranIQ Now 👈**](https://sudosar.github.io/quraniq/)

---

## 🎮 Game Modes

### 🔗 Ayah Connections
Find four groups of four related Quranic items (verses, names, concepts). Group them by specific themes ranging from manifestations of Divine power to historical figures.

### 🔤 Harf by Harf
Guess the Quranic word in six tries. This mode focuses on vocabulary and spelling of key Arabic terms used in the Quran. Use the hints to guide you!

### 🔍 Who Am I?
Identify a Quranic figure or concept based on first-person clues. As you unlock more clues, the challenge decreases—but so does your potential score.

### 🧩 Ayah Scramble
Arrange scrambled words to complete a specific verse. Use the English translations as hints to help you piece the message together.

### 🌙 Juz Journey (Ramadan Special)
A special mode coming for Ramadan that takes you through one Juz each day. Explore themes, listen to recitations, and understand the structure of the Quran.

## ✨ Features Built For You

- **Daily Refresh**: New puzzles every single day to keep your learning consistent.
- **Installable (PWA)**: Add QuranIQ to your phone's home screen for an app-like experience and quick access.
- **Listen & Learn**: Play beautiful audio recitations from professional Qaris as you solve puzzles.
- **Community Leaderboards**: Create or join a group to compete with your friends and family.
- **Save Your Progress**: Generate a secure save code to move your statistics, scores, and streaks between your phone and computer.
- **Deep Study Links**: Every verse you solve provides direct links to Quran.com so you can read the tafsir and reflect deeper.
